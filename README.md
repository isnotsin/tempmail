This bot provides temporary and permanent email solutions with a focus on privacy and control.

As a free user, you get access to essential tools for managing temporary and shared emails.

### Temporary Emails (`/temp`)
-   **Instant Creation:** Instantly create a temporary, disposable email address.
-   **Usage Limit:** You can have up to 3 active temporary emails at a time.
-   **Use Cases:** Perfect for website sign-ups, service verifications, and protecting your primary inbox from spam.

### Shared Email Access (`/login`)
-   **How it Works:** Gain access to a permanent email address created by an Admin or PRO user.
-   **Secure Login:** Requires the full email address and a unique login code provided by the creator (e.g., `/login email@domain.com code`).
-   **Shared Inbox:** The inbox is shared among all users who have the correct login credentials.

### View & Manage Emails (`/myemails`)
-   **Dashboard:** See a list of all your active temporary and shared (logged-in) emails.
-   **Control:** From this list, you have options to:
    -   **Delete** your temporary emails.
    -   **Leave** a shared email, which revokes your access to its inbox.
